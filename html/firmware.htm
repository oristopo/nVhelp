click here for some firmware naming conventions A typical name is NanoVNA-H_900_ch_20191003.dfu
NanoVNA-H
owner or special purpose. edy255 "owns" NanoVNA; Gen Hu "owns" NanoVNA-H
900
most devices can overclock for 900MHz third harmonic. To avoid artifacts with others, use 800
aa
"Antenna Analyzer" limited to 2 traces. Use ch for 4 traces, but smaller text
20191003
date code; newer is not always better. Some group message report bugs..
.dfu
format for DFU utilities, e.g. verify that firmware matches USB device ID
.hex is smaller, lacks ID matching. DFU utility can convert either way.


DMR-CLEAR_MEMORY_DFU.dfu clears device flash memory.
Your device will not again work until after re-load other firmware.
It is used to "clean the slate" prior to installing new firmware.
Some firmware installations fail until after first loading "DMR-CLEAR_MEMORY_DFU.dfu"
